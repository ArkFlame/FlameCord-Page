Choosing the Right Proxy: A Head-to-Head Comparison

In the world of Minecraft server management, choosing the right proxy is a critical decision that impacts performance, security, and player experience. While Velocity is a popular modern proxy, FlameCord, a highly optimized BungeeCord fork, offers a superior blend of features, security, and raw performance. 1. Unmatched Security and Anti-Bot

This is where FlameCord truly shines. While Velocity offers basic security features, FlameCord is a security-hardened solution built specifically to combat modern threats.

  • FlameCord: Comes with a comprehensive, built-in anti-bot suite that includes reconnect detection, country filtering, proxy detection, and an intelligent lockdown mechanism. These tools are integrated at the core level for maximum efficiency.
  • Velocity: Relies on third-party plugins for advanced anti-bot protection. This can lead to compatibility issues, performance overhead, and a slower response to new types of attacks.

2. Superior Performance and Efficiency

Both proxies are designed for performance, but they approach it differently. FlameCord leverages the mature foundation of BungeeCord and enhances it with modern optimizations like Libdeflate compression, resulting in lower CPU and RAM usage under heavy load, as shown in our benchmarks.

  • FlameCord: Optimized for low-latency packet handling and efficient memory management. Its lightweight nature ensures that your server's resources are dedicated to gameplay, not the proxy.
  • Velocity: While fast, its modern architecture can sometimes introduce higher baseline resource consumption compared to a finely-tuned BungeeCord fork.

3. Seamless Compatibility and Ease of Use

FlameCord maintains 100% compatibility with the vast ecosystem of BungeeCord plugins. This means you can switch to FlameCord without abandoning your favorite plugins and tools.

  • FlameCord: As a BungeeCord fork, it's a simple drop-in replacement. Your existing plugins, configurations, and management workflows will work without modification.
  • Velocity: Requires its own specific ecosystem of plugins. While many popular plugins have a Velocity version, you may find that some niche or essential BungeeCord plugins are not available, forcing you to find alternatives or abandon functionality.
